Our Story

Welcome to HQ Ranch Encounters

Located in Claremore, Oklahoma, HQ Ranch Encounters is home to an incredible collection of exotic animals. We started with a passion for animals and a belief that everyone deserves the chance to get up close with wildlife in a safe, caring environment.

Today we're proud to be Oklahoma's largest camel ranch — and so much more. From kangaroos and lemurs to sloths, fennec foxes, and our newest star, Waffles the giraffe, every visit is a one-of-a-kind experience.

We believe in responsible animal care, and every member of our team is dedicated to the health and happiness of the animals in our care.
